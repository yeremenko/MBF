Faina Rudshteyn

Born and raised in London, England, Kerry-Lou now calls New York City home. She has enjoyed a career that has spanned twenty five years, and has had a make-up studio in Chelsea for the last twelve years.


Ever since she was a young child she has loved to paint and "doodle". School tests were constantly handed in covered in elaborate line drawings - which is why, she admits, she didn't do very well academically! Her parents had to grab the morning papers quickly every morning before Kerry-lou could deface them with her cartooning! Kerry-Lou feels in retrospect that the lessons that she has learned painting throughout her life have helped immensely when applied to make-up artistry.
